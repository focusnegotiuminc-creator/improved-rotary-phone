# Automation Engine Overview

The Sacred Geometry AI Engine automates research, writing, design generation, and monetization workflows.

## Workflow
1. Update templates and timestamp logs
2. Generate AI chapter and geometry content
3. Export product-ready manuscript assets
4. Trigger publishing and marketing pipelines

## Tracking
- Execution mode: scheduled + manual
- Timestamp format: ISO-8601 UTC
- Change policy: append-only for iterative history
