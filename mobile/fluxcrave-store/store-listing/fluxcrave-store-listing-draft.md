## App name

## Subtitle / short description

## Full description
Flux & Crave is now one tap away. Use the free FluxCrave app experience to browse the menu, jump to ordering, call, get directions, and keep the flavor close on your phone.

Built for iPhone and Android customers who want a fast, clean way to find the next craving without searching through social posts.

## Core features

- Free to download / free to use.
- Menu browsing and search.
- Order path shortcut.
- One-tap call and directions.
- Mobile-first app experience powered by FluxCrave.com.
