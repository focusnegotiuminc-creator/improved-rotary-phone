# Flux & Crave Privacy Policy Draft

_Last updated: 2026-05-21_

Flux & Crave provides a free mobile app/web app experience for menu browsing, ordering links, calling, directions, and business updates.

## Information collected

The app itself is designed to minimize data collection. Basic technical information may be processed by the website host, browser, analytics tools if enabled, and third-party ordering/maps/call services when a user chooses those actions.

## Ordering and third-party services

If users tap ordering, maps, phone, or social links, they may interact with third-party services. Those services process information according to their own privacy policies.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business owner/admin through the official Flux & Crave contact channel.

## Children

The app is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as features are added. Store listings should be updated before any new tracking, account, payment, or notification feature goes live.
