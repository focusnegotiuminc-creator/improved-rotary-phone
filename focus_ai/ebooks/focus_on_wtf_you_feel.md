# Focus On WTF You FEEL
**A Man's Emotional Intelligence Handbook (Draft)**

## Quick Thesis
Emotions are signals, not enemies. Master the signal, master the decision.

## Framework: P.A.U.S.E.
- **P**ause body reaction
- **A**cknowledge feeling name
- **U**nderstand trigger story
- **S**elect response on values
- **E**xecute with calm language

## Real-World Scenarios
- Conflict with partner.
- Business betrayal.
- Performance anxiety.

## Practical Tools
- 90-second nervous-system reset.
- Trigger journal.
- Boundary scripts.
