# Focus on Foundation (Draft)

## Mission
Help families and founders establish resilient legal, financial, and operational structures.

## Components
1. Entity structure basics
2. Governance and decision rights
3. Documentation and compliance cycles
4. Risk controls and succession planning

## Step-by-Step Starter Sequence
- Clarify objectives.
- Select advisors.
- Form entities.
- Build policies.
- Review annually.

> This manuscript is educational and not legal/tax advice.
