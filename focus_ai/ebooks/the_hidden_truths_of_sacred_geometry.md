# The Hidden Truths of Sacred Geometry
**By Reginald Hilton Jr. (Draft Manuscript)**

## Introduction
Sacred geometry is the study of repeating mathematical patterns that appear in nature, architecture, music, and human perception.

## Core Laws and Patterns
1. **Symmetry and Balance**
2. **Proportion (including golden ratio use-cases)**
3. **Harmonic Repetition**
4. **Radial Expansion and Spiral Dynamics**

## Modern Construction Applications
- Façade rhythm and visual coherence.
- Efficient modular grids.
- Human-centered proportions for comfort.
- Wayfinding that reduces cognitive friction.

## Why Knowledge Was Often Hidden
- Specialized guild knowledge.
- Academic fragmentation.
- Commercial prioritization over meaning.

## Practical Field Guide
- Start with proportional grids.
- Pair geometry with material constraints.
- Validate with cost, structural, and user outcomes.
