# Daily Business Checklist

## Morning
- Review Stripe balance, payments, and failed checkouts.
- Check inbound leads and reply to any sales or support messages.
- Post one free promotion asset on a public platform.
- Review site health for `/`, `/products.html`, `/ebooks/index.html`, and `/landing.html`.

## Sales
- Record each sale source in `sales_tracking_template.csv`.
- Follow up with warm leads who clicked but did not purchase.
- Review the eBook bundle, blueprint pack, and business engine conversion counts.

## Delivery
- Fulfill purchased digital products.
- Confirm buyers received the correct links and onboarding materials.
- Update the lead magnet and email sequence copy if objections repeat.

## Team Ops
- Collect employee hours in `employee_hours_template.csv`.
- Approve hours before payroll cutoff.
- Review open tasks, blockers, and owner assignments for the next work block.

## End Of Day
- Note wins, blockers, and next actions.
- Archive campaign metrics and any pricing or copy changes.
- Rebuild the public site if source content changed.
