# IP & Compliance Checklist

## Important
This is process guidance, not legal advice.

## Brand/IP Protection Steps
1. Record authorship and publication dates for each asset.
2. Save source files and revision history.
3. Register trademarks and copyrights through official government channels.
4. Keep assignment/licensing agreements in writing.
5. Maintain a centralized IP ledger.

## "Free copyright" clarification
Copyright generally attaches automatically upon creation in many jurisdictions, but registration may be required for stronger enforcement options.
