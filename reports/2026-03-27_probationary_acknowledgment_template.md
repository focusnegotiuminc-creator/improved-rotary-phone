# Probationary Period Acknowledgment Template

This is a general business draft, not legal advice.

## Suggested Clause

The first 30 calendar days of employment are considered an introductory probationary period. During that time, job duties, attendance, communication, work quality, and overall fit may be reviewed more closely. Employment may remain at-will to the extent allowed by applicable law and company policy.

Nothing in this acknowledgment permits the company to withhold wages already earned for time worked. Final pay, if employment ends, will be handled according to applicable law and the company’s payroll procedures.

## Why This Version Is Safer

- It creates a probationary period
- It avoids saying earned wages are forfeited
- It leaves final-pay timing tied to actual law and payroll procedure
