# Rent-House Task Brief

## Objective
Identify, contact, and track viable house rental options with a documented outreach workflow.

## Action Checklist
- [ ] Define budget, city/area, bed/bath minimums, and move-in target date.
- [ ] Build shortlist of 10 rental listings.
- [ ] Send inquiry message to each listing.
- [ ] Track responses and schedule viewings.
- [ ] Select top 3 options and compare terms.

## Outreach Prompt Template
Subject: Rental Inquiry

Hello,

I am interested in this rental and would like to confirm availability, monthly rent, deposit amount, lease term, and move-in date.

Please let me know the next steps and any application requirements.

Thank you.
