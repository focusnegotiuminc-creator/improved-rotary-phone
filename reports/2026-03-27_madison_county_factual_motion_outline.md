# Factual Motion Outline

Use this only as a drafting aid while filling out the official Illinois or Madison County motion form.

## Suggested Title

`Motion for Status Hearing and for Leave to Present Supporting Documentation`

If the clerk confirms a different motion is needed, change the title to match the official form guidance.

## Factual Outline

1. Identify yourself, case number, and the current known status.
2. State that you are requesting a hearing and an opportunity to present supporting documentation.
3. State that you previously submitted a continuance request and affidavit because you were unable to appear.
4. State whether you have proof of insurance for the relevant date and want leave to present it.
5. State the facts regarding the vehicle stopping and why it could not be moved immediately, if true.
6. Ask the court to set a hearing date and review the supporting materials.

## Sample Neutral Language

I respectfully request a status hearing and leave to present supporting documentation regarding the matters currently reflected in the docket. I previously attempted to notify the court that I was unable to appear and submitted a continuance request and affidavit. I also request an opportunity to present documentation relevant to the insurance issue and the circumstances surrounding the vehicle stopping at the location listed in the citation materials. I ask the Court to set this matter for hearing and grant any further relief the Court deems just.

## Attachments To Consider

- Copy of your filed continuance request
- Affidavit you previously submitted
- Proof of insurance if applicable
- Timeline of events
- Any repair, tow, or battery-related records
