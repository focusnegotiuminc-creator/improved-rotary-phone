# Market Plan

## Revenue Model
Revenue = Value × Trust × Distribution × Volume.

## Weekly Targets
- $20 books: target 250-500 sales/week (requires strong distribution and conversion testing).
- $99-$299 products: target 20-50 sales/week through bundled offers and service ladders.

## 4-Week Execution
- Week 1: Launch 10 books, upload 5 products, post 20 videos.
- Week 2: Bundle products, add upsells, increase posting cadence.
- Week 3: Scale traffic using best-performing hooks and creator collaborations.
- Week 4: Optimize funnels and retention offers for consistency.
